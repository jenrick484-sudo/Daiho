ABOUT US

Daiho Auto Parts Supply
Located in Kihare, Tankulan, Manolo Fortich, Bukidnon, Daiho Auto Parts Supply is more than just an auto parts store it is a lifeline for drivers, mechanics, and vehicle enthusiasts who seek quality, reliability, and expert service. With a mission to keep vehicles running at peak performance, Daiho Auto Parts Supply is a trusted authorized dealer of premium auto components, catering to brands like Suzuki, Ford, Toyota, Nissan, Honda, Mitsubishi, Hyundai, Volvo, and Fuso.

Daiho Auto Parts Supply is driven by a commitment to quality and customer satisfaction. Every product on the shelves—from essential engine components to lubricants, tires, and accessories—reflects a dedication to durability, performance, and precision. Whether it's a professional mechanic looking for a critical repair part or a vehicle owner searching for routine maintenance supplies, Daiho ensures that every customer drives away with confidence.
