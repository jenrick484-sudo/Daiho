AIR INTAKE SYSTEM

The Air Intake System is responsible for delivering clean, filtered air to a vehicleâ€™s engine for the combustion process. It ensures the engine receives the right amount of oxygen needed for efficient fuel burning and optimal performance.

Common Issues in the Air Intake System:

1. Clogged Air Filter:
  • Reduced airflow can cause poor fuel economy, reduced power, and increased emissions.
2. Dirty or Faulty MAF Sensor:
  • Incorrect air measurement can lead to improper fuel injection and rough engine performance.
3. Vacuum Leaks:
  • Cracks in hoses or connections can allow unmeasured air into the system, leading to erratic idling and engine performance issues.
4. Throttle Body Problems:
  • Carbon build-up or malfunctioning throttle can restrict airflow and affect acceleration.

Tips no.2

Tips no.4
